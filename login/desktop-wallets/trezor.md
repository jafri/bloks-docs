# Trezor

Trezor is a hardware wallet to store your crypto offline rather than online. Currently they support;

* Bitcoin
* Litecoin
* Bitcoin Cash
* Bitcoin Gold
* Dash
* Zcash
* NEM
* Ethereum
* Ethereum Classic

{% embed url="https://trezor.io/" %}
